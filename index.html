Native Tools (Features)
Core tools and capabilities built natively within Verifone Central
Administration
Provides merchants with tools to review and manage details related to their organisations — users, points of interaction, digital wallets, blocking rulesets, notifications, webhooks and more.
Advanced Reporting
BI tool embedded in Verifone Central for advanced analytics and business intelligence. Planned as a future enhancement.
Boarding & Compliance
Merchant On-boarding Portal used by Sales Representatives to on-board new prospects through AML/KYC checks. Once approved, merchants are boarded into the Common Portal.
FinOps
Available for internal users — the place from where the accounting team makes payments to merchants in the context of the Verifone Acquiring Platform. Settlements and financial operations.
Payment Tools
Ecom payment tools available via UI and also via API: Payment Link & Virtual Terminal.
Petro
Focuses on hardware and software in and around gas stations. Using Petro Cloud, clients can manage fuel levels, product pricing, cameras and more. Integrated into Verifone Central via RMS.
Transactions & Reporting
Native transaction data and financial reporting tools for monitoring payment activity within the platform.
SSO-Enabled Applications
External Verifone portals and applications accessible via SSO integration
Premier Portal
Specialised business portal accessible via SSO integration.
Sales Connector
Sales tooling integrated via SSO for streamlined access within the platform. Planned as a future enhancement.
VHQ 2.0
Verifone HQ — one of Verifone's oldest systems, used to manage and administer all Terminals. Enables remote software and payment method updates on devices.
